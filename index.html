Mission

What would our streets look like if we designed them not just for individual movement, but for collective connection, dignity, and care? ...

Transportation is not just a technical issue — it’s a social one. The way we design and govern our streets shapes how we relate to one another, whose needs are prioritized, and what kind of futures we make possible. We believe that safe, equitable, and joyful mobility is fundamental to community well-being and to a more peaceful society.
